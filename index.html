Advantages

We do only what is necessary.

Join beta testing, the more feedback - the faster we will understand what organizers and ordinary users need

  • Event management

    Easily organize concerts, tours, meetups, meetings or even educational processes. We will take care of everything else.

  • Integration with payment systems

    For charity concerts and tours, you can connect up to 10 accounts to collect money for a specific purpose without obstacles.

  • Communication with the audience

    In just a few clicks, you can send a newsletter to each user or to an individually selected group.

  • Donate tickets

    We have a handy tool for organizers who want to run a Giveaway or just give someone a ticket.

  • A surprise for the organizers

    After the completed tour, the organizers receive detailed statistics and infographics about their performances.
